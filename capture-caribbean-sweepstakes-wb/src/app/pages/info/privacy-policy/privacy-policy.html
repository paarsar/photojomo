  • @capturecaribbean.sweepstakes (for sweepstakes-specific communications, if applicable)

B. What We Will Never Ask You Via Email

We will never ask you to:

  • Provide your Social Security Number (USA winners will provide this via secure, encrypted document upload, not via email)
  • Provide your passport number or driver’s license number via email
  • Pay any fee to claim a prize (the Sweepstakes requires no payment)
  • Wire money or purchase gift cards
  • Click a link to “verify your account” using sensitive personal information
  • Download any software or attachment to claim a prize

C. How to Report Suspicious Emails

If you receive a suspicious email claiming to be from Capture Caribbean:

  1. Do not click any links or download attachments
  2. Forward the email to phishing@capturecaribbean.com
  3. Delete the email from your inbox and trash
  4. If you have already provided information in response to a suspicious email, contact us immediately at privacy@capturecaribbean.com

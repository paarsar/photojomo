The Saint Lucia Exhibition (2033)

This inaugural exhibition is more than a showcase of photographs — it is an invitation to experience the soul of Saint Lucia. From its lush peaks and vibrant communities to its deep cultural heritage and living traditions, the exhibition will reveal the island not just as a destination, but as a living story told through the eyes of those who have captured its light, legacy, and landscape.

Beginning in Saint Lucia, the exhibition will travel to major cities across the globe in 2034 and beyond, inviting international audiences to step into the heart of the Caribbean. Through this global journey, Saint Lucia’s story will resonate far beyond its shores, connecting cultures and inspiring new ways of seeing, feeling, and understanding the Caribbean.

Global Visions of Saint Lucia

The Capture Saint Lucia Exhibition Tour launches in 2033 on the island where the journey began — Saint Lucia. Featuring the work of invited photo and video artists from around the world, the tour will celebrate the island’s majesty through a curated showcase of imagery, storytelling, and immersive experiences.

Starting in Saint Lucia, the exhibition will travel to major cities across the globe in 2034 and beyond — inviting international audiences to discover Saint Lucia’s soul through the eyes of diverse creatives who were called to capture its light, legacy, and landscape.

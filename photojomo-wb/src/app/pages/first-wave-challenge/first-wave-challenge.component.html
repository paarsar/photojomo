Forge the Founding Class

The first creators to shape the future of Capture Caribbean.

The First Wave Challenge invites global visual storytellers to launch Capture Caribbean as our inaugural Founding Creators. Whether professional or passionate, we are selecting a cohort of artists to define the creative foundation and set the standard for future Invitationals.

Why Become a Founding Creator?

  • Priority consideration for future Invitationals, Exhibitions, and Residencies1
  • Become a part of Global Artist Community.
  • Early platform access.
  • Receive digital badge of founding class member.
  • Lifetime recognition of founding creator.

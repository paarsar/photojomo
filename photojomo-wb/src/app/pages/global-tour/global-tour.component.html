Capture Saint Lucia — The Beginning of Capture Caribbean.

More than landscapes — this is a journey through the heartbeat of the Caribbean: its people, cultures, faiths, and stories. The Capture Caribbean International Tour unites the world through the transformative power of art, human creativity, and ingenuity. And it all begins with Saint Lucia.

 

The Capture Saint Lucia Tour marks the first chapter of this global cultural journey. Known for its breathtaking beauty, vibrant traditions, and deep heritage, Saint Lucia will serve as the opening lens through which the world experiences the soul of the Caribbean. Through photography, film, multimedia installations, and immersive storytelling, the tour will reveal the island’s living culture beyond the postcard image.

Global Vision — Capture Saint Lucia Tour

Each stop on this inaugural tour is an encounter with the many expressions of the human spirit, with the places and communities that hold our stories, and with the shared curiosity and meaning that connect us across borders.

This is the power of art in motion. This is legacy on tour.

The Capture Saint Lucia Photo & Video Contest is an international visual arts competition designed to celebrate the soul of Saint Lucia—its breathtaking landscapes, rich culture, and captivating stories. Capture Saint Lucia™

Capture Saint Lucia™ is the first international visual arts invitational of the Capture Caribbean Initiative, a global cultural movement that amplifies the Caribbean’s artistic voice on the world stage. Through the lens of visual artists, the initiative embraces an Arts First approach — inviting creators across borders, backgrounds, and disciplines to explore Saint Lucia’s landscapes, culture, people, and stories, and to share their vision with the world.

 

More than a competition, Capture Caribbean is the movement, and Capture Saint Lucia™ is its first chapter — the spark that sets the global journey in motion.
The invitational culminates in Capture Saint Lucia — The Exhibition, the first international exhibition of the Capture Caribbean Initiative, launching in Saint Lucia in 2030 before traveling to major cultural capitals around the world.

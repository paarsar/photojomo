About the Vision

More Than an Island — A Caribbean Vision

The Capture Saint Lucia Photo & Video Contest is an international visual arts competition designed to celebrate the soul of Saint Lucia—its breathtaking landscapes, rich culture, and captivating stories. Open to both professional and amateur photographers and filmmakers, this contest invites creatives from around the world to showcase their artistry by capturing the spirit, essence, and untold narratives of this Caribbean gem through photography and videography.

Capture Saint Lucia International Tour

The works created through Capture Saint Lucia will not only debut on the island but will also travel to international cultural capitals. From New York to London, Paris to Toronto, this multi-city exhibition tour will introduce audiences worldwide to Saint Lucia’s stories, beauty, and creative excellence.
